## Call Script (When They Reply)

**Opening (30 seconds)**:
"Hi [Name], thanks for picking up! I'll keep this quick. We built CareMenu for healthcare food services — basically, it tracks HACCP compliance and waste in one system. Most of our customers save £20-30k in year 1. Does that sound interesting?"

**If Yes**: "Great! Quick question — what's your biggest pain right now: compliance prep, waste tracking, or cost control?"

**If No**: "No worries! Would you be open to a 20-minute demo just to see if it makes sense?"

**Demo Talking Points**:
1. HACCP compliance automation (show live compliance tracker)
2. Waste cost dashboard (show calculator)
3. Audit report generation (show sample PDF)
4. Cost: £1,299/month, 30-day free trial
5. Close: "Want to try it free for a month?"

## Success Metrics (Track These)

- Emails sent: 50+
- Response rate: Target 8-10% (4-5 responses)
- Demo booked: Target 3-5
- Close rate: Target 30-50% (1-2 customers)

## Tools to Use

1. **Email tracking**: Mailtrack.io or Streak (see who opens/clicks)
2. **Calendly**: For demo scheduling (embed on landing page)
3. **Airtable/Google Sheet**: Track all prospects + stage
4. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator**: Find targets (optional but helpful)
