# HealthServe Vercel Sales Website Prompt

Copy and paste the prompt below into Vercel's new project flow at `https://vercel.com/new`.

```text
Build a premium sales website for a service company called HealthServe.

Important: this is a service website, not a SaaS product dashboard. Do not build login, app screens, database models, admin panels, or product CRUD workflows. Build a high-converting marketing and sales website designed to generate enquiries, calls, and consultation bookings.

Company name:
HealthServe

What HealthServe does:
HealthServe helps care homes, hospitals, healthcare kitchens, and regulated food service operators reduce food waste, improve catering operations, simplify compliance preparation, and control food service costs.

Positioning:
"Food service improvement for healthcare teams. Reduce waste, tighten compliance routines, and control kitchen costs without adding admin burden."

Target customers:
- Care homes
- Nursing homes
- Hospitals
- Private healthcare facilities
- Healthcare catering teams
- Regulated institutional kitchens
- Multi-site care groups

Primary buyers:
- Care home owners
- Managing directors
- Operations directors
- Food service directors
- Heads of catering
- Catering managers
- Facilities managers
- Procurement leads

Main customer problems:
- Food waste is too high but not measured properly
- HACCP and food safety records are inconsistent
- Audit preparation is stressful and manual
- Stock and expiry issues cause avoidable loss
- Supplier spending is hard to review
- Kitchen teams rely on spreadsheets and paper
- Managers do not have a clear weekly view of food service performance

Services to sell:
1. Food Waste Reduction Review
   - Review current waste tracking
   - Identify avoidable waste patterns
   - Build a simple waste measurement process
   - Recommend practical savings actions

2. HACCP and Compliance Readiness Review
   - Review current food safety routines
   - Identify gaps in logs, evidence, and staff process
   - Prepare an audit-readiness action plan
   - Improve daily compliance discipline

3. Stock, Expiry, and Supplier Control
   - Review stock control routines
   - Identify expiry risk and over-ordering
   - Review supplier and ordering practices
   - Create a tighter weekly ordering process

4. 30-Day Kitchen Performance Sprint
   - A focused service package for one facility
   - Baseline current waste, stock, compliance, and ordering process
   - Implement simple tracking routines
   - Deliver weekly improvement actions
   - End with a management report and next-step plan

Website goal:
Generate qualified leads and booked consultations.

Primary CTA:
Book a 20-minute consultation

Secondary CTA:
Request a food waste review

Design direction:
- Professional healthcare operations feel
- Clean, credible, direct, and service-led
- White, slate, blue, and subtle green accents
- No playful startup visuals
- No generic stock imagery unless relevant to healthcare catering or professional kitchens
- Use crisp sections, strong headings, concise copy, and clear CTAs
- Make it feel trustworthy for care home and healthcare decision-makers
- Mobile responsive
- Fast-loading
- Accessible contrast

Build with:
- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- App Router if suitable
- Static-first website
- Contact/lead form
- No backend database unless needed for the form
- If form handling is included, make it easy to connect to email, Resend, Formspree, or Vercel serverless functions

Required pages:
1. Home page
2. Services page
3. 30-Day Sprint page
4. About page
5. Contact page
6. Privacy page

Home page sections:
- Hero with strong headline, subheadline, and two CTAs
- Trust bar for target sectors: care homes, hospitals, healthcare kitchens, regulated catering
- Problem section: waste, compliance, stock, suppliers, audit prep
- Services overview with 3-4 service cards
- 30-Day Kitchen Performance Sprint feature section
- Results section with outcome-focused metrics:
  - Reduce avoidable food waste
  - Improve audit readiness
  - Build clearer weekly stock controls
  - Give managers better visibility
- Process section:
  1. Review current operation
  2. Identify waste and compliance gaps
  3. Implement simple tracking routines
  4. Deliver management action plan
- CTA section to book a consultation
- Footer with contact details, services, and legal links

Hero copy direction:
Headline options:
- "Reduce healthcare kitchen waste without adding more admin."
- "Food service improvement for care homes and healthcare kitchens."
- "Make food waste, compliance, stock, and supplier control easier to manage."

Subheadline:
HealthServe helps healthcare food service teams find avoidable waste, tighten daily compliance routines, and build practical operating controls that managers can actually use.

Service page requirements:
- Explain each service clearly
- Include who it is for
- Include what is reviewed
- Include what the customer receives
- Include CTA after each service
- Avoid vague consulting language

30-Day Sprint page requirements:
Create a dedicated sales page for the "30-Day Kitchen Performance Sprint".

Sections:
- Hero: clear offer and outcome
- Who it is for
- What happens week by week:
  - Week 1: baseline review
  - Week 2: waste, stock, and compliance tracking improvements
  - Week 3: supplier, ordering, and expiry control
  - Week 4: management report and next-step operating plan
- Deliverables:
  - Waste review summary
  - Compliance readiness checklist
  - Stock and expiry risk review
  - Supplier/order process notes
  - Management action plan
- CTA: "Book a 20-minute consultation"

About page requirements:
- Position HealthServe as practical, operations-focused, and built for healthcare food service environments
- Emphasise measurable improvement, simple routines, and management visibility
- Do not make unsupported claims like "500+ customers" unless framed as example placeholders

Contact page requirements:
- Include a lead form with:
  - Name
  - Organisation
  - Role
  - Email
  - Phone
  - Facility type
  - Main issue dropdown: waste, compliance, stock/expiry, supplier costs, audit prep, other
  - Message
- Include CTA copy:
  "Tell us what is happening in your kitchen. We will reply with the best next step."
- Include placeholder contact email: hello@healthserve.co

SEO requirements:
- Page title: HealthServe | Healthcare Food Service Improvement
- Meta description: HealthServe helps care homes, hospitals, and healthcare kitchens reduce food waste, improve compliance routines, and control food service costs.
- Use semantic headings
- Include local/UK-friendly language where natural, including care homes, CQC preparation, HACCP routines, and healthcare catering

Conversion requirements:
- Every main page should have a clear CTA above the fold
- Repeat CTA near the bottom
- Use practical benefit-led copy
- Make the contact path obvious
- Include "Book a 20-minute consultation" buttons
- Include "Request a food waste review" buttons

Do not:
- Do not build an app dashboard
- Do not include login/register
- Do not include SaaS pricing tiers
- Do not include database schema or Prisma
- Do not include fake customer logos
- Do not make exaggerated unsupported claims
- Do not mention old names like StockSense or CareMenu
- Do not make the site feel like a generic template

Output:
Create the complete website with polished copy, responsive UI, reusable components, and clear file structure. The result should be ready to deploy on Vercel as a sales website for HealthServe.
```

```text
Improve this HealthServe website so it feels more like a specialist healthcare food service improvement consultancy and less like a generic SaaS landing page.

Make the copy sharper, more operational, and more specific to care homes, hospitals, HACCP routines, CQC preparation, waste tracking, stock control, expiry risk, supplier ordering, and management visibility.

Remove any login, dashboard, app, software-product, or fake-metric language. The goal is sales enquiries for a service business. Strengthen the CTAs, service descriptions, 30-day sprint page, and contact form.
```
