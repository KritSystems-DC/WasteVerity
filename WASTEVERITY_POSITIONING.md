# WasteVerity Brand Positioning

## Audience

WasteVerity is for UK healthcare and regulated food service operators that need tighter control over kitchen waste, stock, compliance records, and management reporting.

Primary audiences:
- Care home owners, managers, and catering leads.
- Hospital and private healthcare food service teams.
- Multi-site operators with central oversight responsibilities.
- Regulated kitchens that need consistent records for inspections and internal audits.

## Pain Points

- Food waste is recorded inconsistently, so managers cannot see the true cost or cause.
- Stock, expiry dates, suppliers, staff requests, and reorder decisions live in spreadsheets, notebooks, or disconnected systems.
- Compliance evidence is time-consuming to prepare before CQC, FSA, HACCP, or internal reviews.
- Kitchen teams need simple daily workflows, not another complex admin system.
- Leadership needs clearer reporting on waste loss, expiry exposure, low stock, and operational risk.

## Offer

WasteVerity gives regulated kitchens one operating system for waste tracking, inventory control, compliance records, staff requests, suppliers, reorders, and reporting.

Core promise:
Reduce avoidable food waste, simplify audit preparation, and make stock control easier to manage from one shared workflow.

Launch offer:
A 30-day single-facility pilot that configures inventory categories, waste reasons, compliance tasks, users, supplier records, and management reports.

## Proof Points

- Tracks waste by item, quantity, reason, site, and estimated cost.
- Connects stock levels, expiry dates, reorder thresholds, and supplier costs.
- Supports HACCP-ready operational records and inspection preparation.
- Gives staff a structured route for missing or low-stock item requests.
- Exports stock, waste, reorder, and reporting data for management review.
- Positioned around practical outcomes: 15-20% target food waste reduction, less compliance admin, and clearer facility-level visibility.
